About Us?

Perfect Match Jakarta (PMJ) was founded in 2013 for the purpose of providing a trustworthy online dating solution to eligible unattached business professionals.

Our aim is to provide the necessary support to our valued members in order to safely experience a new dating concept that can enhance expectations and possibilities while finding a potential partner through a practical approach. PMJ has developed a simplified dating engine that connects participating members by matching their personality traits and unique preferences with other like-minded potential partners, thus creating the Perfect Match!

Our emphasis while creating PMJ remained focused on developing a system where we can connect people within a safe and secure platform.

Our safe and secure system protocols protects personal information, so that members will be able to connect with each other anonymously. Knowing that no personal information will ever be shared without their approval and that their identities will be protected from starting point.

Once you feel more comfortable, and at your own discretion, you may reveal your personal information in order to pursue a potential Perfect Match. Discover what you have been missing and let Perfect Match Jakarta help you bring positive possibilities into your personal life.
